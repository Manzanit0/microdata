Dessert

Vanilla panna cotta

Products Used

  • Natrel Lactose Free 35% Whipping Cream

  • Natrel Lactose Free 2%

Type and click enter
Close

The perfect balance between sweet and light for your dessert. This classic vanilla panna cotta will definitely impress your guests!

Serves Preparation time Cooking time Cooling time
6 10min. 5min. 180min.

Ingredients

  • 2 sheets of gelatin
  • 1 cup (250 mL) Natrel Lactose free 2%
  • 1 cup (250 mL) Natrel Lactose free 35% whipping cream
  • 1 tbsp. vanilla extract
  • 3 generous tablespoons of sugar

Preparation

  1. Soften the gelatin sheets in a bowl of cold water.
  2. Pour the milk and cream into a saucepan. Add the sugar and vanilla, and then mix it all together. Bring it to a boil and remove from heat.
  3. Squeeze the water out of the gelatin sheets and add them to the preparation.
  4. Stir with a wooden spoon or a whisk to incorporate the gelatin.
  5. Pour the mixture into verrines or small ramekins.
  6. Let them cool to room temperature before refrigerating for a few hours.
